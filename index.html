About Rent a Maid

Rent a Maid India was founded with a simple mission: to connect busy urban families with reliable, verified, and professional domestic help. We understand that inviting someone into your home requires absolute trust.

Our rigorous background checks, comprehensive training programs, and transparent pricing ensure that both our customers and our helpers are treated with respect and fairness.

Over 8 years, we've helped 12,000+ families across 100+ cities find the right domestic help — from maids and cooks to nurses, babysitters, and drivers. Every helper in our network is verified for identity, background, medical fitness, and skill.

We believe domestic workers deserve dignity, fair pay, and safe working conditions. Our platform is designed to protect both parties — with transparent contracts, clear terms, and a dedicated support team available 6 days a week.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services, pricing, and safety measures.

We typically place a helper within 24–72 hours of receiving your requirement. For specialized roles like nurses or cooks, it may take 3–5 days to find the perfect match.
Every helper goes through police verification, Aadhar verification, address verification, medical fitness check, and a skills assessment before joining our platform. We also collect references from previous employers.
We offer a 30-day free replacement guarantee on all placements. If you're not satisfied with the helper within the first 30 days, we will find a replacement at no additional cost. Premium plans get a 6-month replacement guarantee.
We charge a one-time placement fee equal to 15 days of the finalized monthly salary. This covers sourcing, background verification, medical screening, and the replacement guarantee. There are no monthly subscription fees.
Yes, we have both live-in and live-out options available. Live-in helpers are provided accommodation and meals by the employer, and their salary range is typically higher. We recommend a clear written agreement outlining duties, rest days, and salary.
We currently serve 100+ cities across India, including all metros, Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities. Visit our Cities page to check availability in your area. If your city isn't listed, contact us — we may still be able to help.
You can terminate the arrangement with one month's notice (or salary in lieu). We recommend a written notice. If the helper did something serious, please contact us immediately — we have a grievance redressal process in place.
